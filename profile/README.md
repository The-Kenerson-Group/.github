## The Kenerson Group

### The Kenerson Group (TKG) has been at for forefront of geographic information systems software and consulting services for forestry and landscape management since 1997. Driven by a unique partnership of GIS and forestry/arboriculture professionals, our principal staff members are active in their professional communities, and always accessible to our clients.

### TreeWorks™ tree management software was developed by a partnership of GIS experts and consulting arborists. The software runs as an extension to ESRI’s™ ArcGIS Pro™. TreeWorks’ mobile component can run on any device. You can collect data with traditional windows-based devices or edit your tree inventory on-the-fly with your Android or Apple phone or tablet with ArcGIS Field Maps™.
